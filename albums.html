Ed Sheeran Albums

Ed Sheeran released his debut studio album in 2011 titled "+". Since then, he has become one of the best-selling music artists in the world. This page showcases his greatest achievements by highlighting his top 3 albums ranked in order.




Ed Sheeran's top 3 albums are ranked as follows: At number 1 is "÷" (Divide), followed by "x" (Multiply), and then his debut album "+" (Plus).

Explanations of the Albums

÷ (Divide)
- Featuring global hits like “Shape of You” and “Perfect,” this album cemented Sheeran’s status as a pop sensation. It showcases his versatility with upbeat pop tracks and emotional ballads.

x (Multiply)
- With songs like “Thinking Out Loud” and “Photograph,” this album demonstrated his lyrical depth and ability to craft timeless love songs with soulful melodies and heartfelt storytelling.

+ (Plus)
- This debut album introduced the world to Ed’s unique blend of acoustic folk and heartfelt lyrics. Tracks like “The A Team” and “Lego House” made him a breakout star.
